About Me

I’m still new to programming. I only started a couple of years ago, but it’s quickly become something I really enjoy.

Before this, I spent a lot of time making music. I mainly produced trance and progressive house, but I also really enjoyed creating game music on the side.

When I was younger, I used to make small games in RPG Maker XP with my friends. Back then, I never thought I would actually get the chance to work with games in a real way.

In many ways, this feels like a childhood dream I didn’t expect to come back.

What I’ve come to enjoy the most is working with AI and gameplay systems, especially when behaviour starts to feel alive on screen. I like building systems that are easy to understand and work with, even if I’m still learning how to do that properly.

I’m curious, calm, and enjoy figuring things out step by step. I like understanding how systems fit together and improving them through iteration.

If you want to talk about game development, music production, or progressive house, feel free to reach out.
