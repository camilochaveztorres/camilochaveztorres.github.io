About Me

Salutations!

I’m Camilo and I'm currently studying Game Programming at The Game Assembly in Malmö.

I started programming a couple of years ago and it quickly became something I really enjoy.

Before that, I spent a lot of time making music. I mainly produced trance and progressive house but I also made some game music on the side.

When I was younger I used to make small games in RPG Maker XP with my friends. At the time I never thought I’d actually get the chance to work with games in a more serious way. Working with games now kind of feels like a childhood dream come true.

I’m mainly interested in AI and gameplay systems, especially when behavior starts to feel alive on screen. I enjoy building systems, figuring out how different parts connect and then improving them step by step.

I would describe myself as calm and curious. I like taking things apart to get a better understanding of how they work.

If you want to talk about game development, music production or progressive house, feel free to reach out.
